Mango Chia Pudding

Recipe

Chia seeds are an absolute blessing for anyone who is following a paleo diet. Not only are these wonderful little seeds high in protein, fiber, and healthy omega-3 fats, but they can also be added to breakfast smoothies for an added health boost, or my personal favorite, used to make many different types of delicious paleo-friendly chia seed puddings. This yummy mango chia pudding can be enjoyed at any time of day, whether as a healthy breakfast option, a midday snack, or as a delightful dessert after dinner. It takes no time at all to prepare; simply spend 10 minutes putting the ingredients together followed by one hour in the fridge and youâ€™re all set to enjoy this delicious chia pudding.

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup full-fat coconut milk
  2. 3 tbsp of chia seeds
  3. 2 cups of chopped mango
  4. 2 tbsp pure maple syrup

Recipe

  1. In a jar, mix coconut milk, chia seeds, and 1 tablespoon of honey.
  2. Cover and refrigerate for an hour.
  3. In a blender, combine mango and 1 tablespoon of honey and blend until smooth.
  4. Layer chia pudding and pureed mango evenly among 4 small glasses.
  5. Garnish with coconut flake (if using) and serve.
